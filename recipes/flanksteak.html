Marinated Flank Steak

A great flank steak marinade like this one is important if you want a tender, juicy, flavorful steak. Make sure you marinate your flank steak for at least 2 hours for best results or longer if you have time. This wonderful quick and easy recipe also works great when the steak is sliced and used for fajitas.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb flank steak
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  2. In a small bowl, mix together olive oil, soy sauce, Worcestershire sauce, honey, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper.
  3. Place flank steak in a large resealable plastic bag and pour marinade over the steak. Seal the bag and massage the steak to coat it evenly with the marinade.
  4. Place the steak in a large resealable plastic bag and pour marinade over the steak. Seal the bag and massage the steak to coat it evenly with the marinade.
  5. Place the steak in a large resealable plastic bag and pour marinade over the steak. Seal the bag and massage the steak to coat it evenly with the marinade.
  6. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the steak and cook until browned on all sides, about 5 minutes per side.
  7. Remove the steak from the skillet and set it aside.
  8. In the same skillet, add the remaining marinade and cook, stirring constantly, until the sauce has thickened and the steak is coated, about 5 minutes.
  9. Add the steak back to the skillet and cook until browned on all sides, about 5 minutes per side.
