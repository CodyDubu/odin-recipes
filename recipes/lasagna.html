Lasagna

A classic Italian dish that is easy to make and always a crowd-pleaser. This recipe is a bit more complicated than most, but it's worth it. It's also a great way to use up leftover lasagna noodles.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb lasagna noodles
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh basil
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 2 tbsp olive oil

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350Â°F
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package directions. Drain and rinse under cold water.
  3. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium-high heat. Add garlic and cook until fragrant, about 1 minute.
  4. Add the noodles to the skillet and toss to coat with the garlic. Cook until the noodles are al dente, about 8 minutes.
  5. In a large bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, mozzarella cheese, Parmesan cheese, basil, parsley, salt, and pepper. Toss to combine.
  6. Spread the ricotta mixture in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  7. Arrange a layer of lasagna noodles on top of the ricotta mixture.
  8. Spread a layer of the remaining ricotta mixture over the noodles.
  9. Repeat layers of noodles and ricotta mixture until all ingredients are used up.
  10. Top with shredded mozzarella cheese.
  11. Bake for 30 minutes.
  12. Let cool for 10 minutes before slicing and serving.
